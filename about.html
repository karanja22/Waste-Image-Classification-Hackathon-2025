How the Model Works

The classifier was trained using an extensive dataset of waste images.
It learns to distinguish between organic and recyclable waste using image patterns.
It receives the uploaded image, then predicts whether it is recyclable or organic.

Why classifying waste matters

Classifying waste is essential for a sustainable future. Here are some benefits of classifying waste:

  • It enables efficient recycling.
  • It reduces the volume of waste sent to landfills.
  • It promotes resource conservation.
  • It minimizes environmental pollution.

By sorting waste correctly, we reduce landfill pollution, improve recycling efficiency, and contribute to a sustainable future.
